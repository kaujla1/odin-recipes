Alfredo Recipe

What is alfredo?

Fettuccine Alfredo or fettuccine al burro is an Italian pasta dish of fresh fettuccine tossed with butter and Parmesan cheese. As the cheese melts, it emulsifies the liquids to form a smooth and rich cheese sauce coating the pasta.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Butter
  • 1 1/2 Cups Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 2 Teaspoons Garlic Minced
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Italian Seasoning
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Pepper
  • 2 Cups Freshly Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 Pack Fettuccini

Cooking Directions

  1. Start boiling water for noodles
  2. Add noodles once water is boiling
  3. Add the butter and cream to a large skillet
  4. Simmer over low heat for 2 minutes
  5. Whisk in the garlic, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper for one minute
  6. Whisk in the parmesan cheese until melted
  7. Strain noodles once cooked
  8. Add cooked noodles to sauce and mix
